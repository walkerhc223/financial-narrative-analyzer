Financial Narrative Analyzer
Output Structure v1

Purpose:
Define the structure of the narrative output produced by the system.

1. Executive Summary
- 2 to 4 sentences
- Summarize overall period performance
- Emphasize the highest-priority material signal
- Mention whether profitability improved or worsened

2. Revenue Commentary
- State current vs prior revenue
- Include absolute and percentage change
- Indicate whether change was favorable or unfavorable
- Briefly note whether revenue was a major driver

3. Gross Profit Commentary
- State current vs prior gross profit
- Include absolute and percentage change
- Mention gross margin expansion or compression if material
- Interpret whether profitability at the gross level improved or worsened

4. Operating Expense Commentary
- State current vs prior operating expense
- Include absolute and percentage change
- Explain whether expense growth supported or pressured profitability
- Reference management notes only if provided

5. Operating Income Conclusion
- State current vs prior operating income
- Include absolute and percentage change
- Identify the main reason operating income changed
- Summarize the core performance story

6. Key Watchout (Optional)
- Highlight one important caution or monitoring point
- Only include if supported by the computed signals or management notes
