What specific problem does the Financial Narrative Analyzer solve?
Obtaining a summary of Financial Statements based on limited information

Who is the user?
Management in the Finance and other departments 

What kind of financial input will it analyze first?
Period-over-period changes in key income statement line items

What kind of narrative output should it produce?
Concise narrative summary

What is explicitly out of scope for version 1?
Forecasting, valuation, external benchmarking, or broad strategic analysis.
