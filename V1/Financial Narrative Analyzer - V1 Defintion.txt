Financial Narrative Analyzer — V1 Definition

The Financial Narrative Analyzer is a tool that converts structured period-over-period income statement metrics into standardized, management-ready performance narratives. It reduces the manual effort and variability involved in interpreting financial results and communicating business performance.

Primary users are finance professionals preparing financial commentary, while secondary users are non-finance managers consuming performance explanations.

Version 1 analyzes numeric inputs for revenue, gross profit, operating expenses, and operating income across two reporting periods.

The system produces concise, structured narratives explaining performance drivers, directional trends, and key profitability impacts.

Version 1 does not include forecasting, valuation, benchmarking, strategic analysis, multi-period trend modeling, balance sheet or cash flow analysis, or unstructured financial document interpretation.
